Geo-Appsmith is a geospatial consulting and software development company that leverages on Earth Observation Data (EO), Cloud Computing, Geospatial AI and Machine Learning to create impactful solutions for our rapidly changing planet.

Our Engineers have built powerful tools that deliver meaningful insights at a global scale, to support informed decision making for organizations such as Conservation International Foundation(CI), The Group on Earth Observation (GEO),  International Union for Conservation of Nature(IUCN), Deutsche Stiftung Weltbevölkerung (DSW), among others.

We pair talented and passionate engineers and developers with problems that are exciting and impactful. 

About Us

Welcome to the project that 'escaped' the lab

How it started:

Geo-Appsmith was established in the year 2016 with a mission to create and deploy intuitive and simplified Web-GIS and Mobile Mapping solutions to enable organizations and communities to easily and quickly make informed decisions with spatial data.

One of our earliest partners was Luciad (before the Belgian company was acquired by Hexagon). During the time, we helped in shipping Luciad's powerful geospatial  awareness software tools to the East African Market.

Today: 

Today, our skilled software engineers continue to design, develop and deploy location based solutions for organizations in many sectors; Health, Agriculture, Environment, Transport & Logistics, Telecommunication, Education, Forestry, etc. 

We have managed to touch a nerve and cut a niche in the market by creating jaw-dropping solutions for many organizations such as Conservation International Foundation (CI), The International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN), The Group on Earth Observation (GEO), Soulco Kenya, among others.

Our Expertise

GEO PRODUCTS

We build intuitive interfaces that enable users to interact and derive insights from spatial data. Our jaw-dropping designs empower decision makers and doers to visualize, probe, analyze, and to act. Our approach is firmly based in the principles of user centered, participatory design, simplicity and results.

CLOUD GEO

We leverage on the power of cloud computing (and GEE)  to build infrastructure that power mission critical operations so users, researchers, and decision makers can focus on the complexities of the problem they are trying to solve without worrying about servers, building secure APIs, and worrying about how to scale.

GEO AI

We develop intelligent computer programs to mimic processes of human perception, spatial reasoning, and discovery about geographical phenomena and dynamics; to solve problems in environmental systems and their interactions, with a focus on spatial contexts and roots in geographic information science.
